Security Practices to Stay Safe Online

Use Strong Passwords

Ensure your passwords are unique, complex, and at least 12 characters long. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters. Consider using a password manager to keep track of your passwords.

Avoid Suspicious Links

Never click on links or download attachments from unknown or unexpected emails or messages. Always verify the source before interacting with any online content.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Use 2FA wherever possible to add an extra layer of security to your accounts. This ensures that even if your password is compromised, your account remains protected.

Keep Your Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system, browser, and apps to ensure you have the latest security patches and fixes, reducing vulnerabilities to attacks.
